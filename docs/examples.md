### Simple Pancake Recipe

```txt
Title: Simple Pancake Recipe
Author: Jane Doe
Servings: 4
Time: 10 minutes prep, 15 minutes cook

Tools:
- Mixing bowl
- Whisk
- Non-stick skillet

Ingredients:
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups milk

Instructions:
1. Whisk dry ingredients.
2. Beat eggs and mix wet ingredients.
3. Combine wet and dry.
4. Cook in skillet.
